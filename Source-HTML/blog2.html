Cho tôi xin một vé đi tuổi thơ
- Nguyễn Nhật Ánh -
With “Cho tôi xin một vé đi tuổi thơ” by Nguyễn Nhật Ánh. The author breathes life into the characters throughout his story. From the boy Mui who wants to practice being a "tiny revolutionary", with a rich imagination who always wants to change all the inevitable and boring things in everyday life to the philosophies that sound silly at times, sometimes filled with the depth of children who have not gone through an eighth of their lives.
Mỗi lần vấp ngã là một lần Trưởng Thành
- Liêu Trí Phong -
“Mỗi lần vấp ngã là một lần Trưởng Thành” by Liêu Trí Phong is a good book about life with content in the direction of storytelling and analysis, helping you to develop yourself. In a random visit to a bookstore, I accidentally noticed the book because the title was too good. The stories here are like a concrete example of vividness in the daily lives of each of us, including you and me.
Tuổi Trẻ Đáng Giá Bao Nhiêu?
- Rosie Nguyễn -
"Tuổi trẻ đáng giá bao nhiêu?" is divided into 3 parts by the author: LEARN, DO, GO. But beyond that I found the book to show a fourth part, READ. Read books, if you read books persistently, there will come a time when you will be constantly driven by the desire to write your own book. If I were in my twenties, I would probably read "Tuổi trẻ đáng giá bao nhiêu?" more than once.
