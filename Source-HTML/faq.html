Which countries and regions do you deliver to?

We're sorry if your country or region isn't on the list, we review the list of delivery locations regularly with a view to expanding these where possible.
Please note you are responsible for any local taxes and shipping. For deliveries to EU, the prices on our website and what you pay at checkout is the final price.

My order is processing when will it ship?

If your order is still processing, that means that we are waiting for your item from our suppliers. As soon as it has shipped we will send you an email to let you know.
You can also check your order status here. If you were logged in when you placed the order, you can also have a look at your order history.

When will my order arrive?

Books can take different times to get to you, it really depends on the supplier for each book. Each books detail page will tell you when you should expect delivery for that book.
The estimated delivery time we show on our product pages is based on the country we believe you are in. If you are delivering to another country, the estimated delivery timeframe will be different and once your book/s have been shipped they will normally be delivered within the time listed below.

How do I change the delivery address for my order?

Providing your order hasn't already shipped, please contact us via the online contact form and ensure you include your order number and the new delivery address including your full name, new address, etc ...
Please note that we are unable to amend delivery addresses from one EU country to another EU country for tax reasons.
